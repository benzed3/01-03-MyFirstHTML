How to make a sandwhich

  1. Get bread, lettuce, tomato, mustard, ham, mayo
  2. Get knife
  3. Get two bread slices
  4. Put mayo on two bread slices
  5. Put mustard on bread slices
  6. Put ham on one slice
  7. Put lettuce on one slice
  8. Put tomato on one slice
  9. Put slices together
  10. Eat
